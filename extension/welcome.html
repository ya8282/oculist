Welcome to Oculist

Chrome Extension

Stop squinting. Use Oculist to find text on any page and see it instantly.

What it does

Tired of searching for a text match in a dense sea of text? This extension replaces the subtle default highlight with eye-catching, animated visual beacons. Locate exactly what you’re looking for with style.

How to use it

Open the Find bar with your keyboard shortcut, or click the Oculist icon in your toolbar.

macOS Cmd+Shift+F  or  Cmd+F
Windows / Linux Ctrl+Shift+F  or  Ctrl+F

Type your search, then press Enter (or Shift+Enter to go back) to jump between matches. The current active match activates an animation so that you can spot it instantly.

Make it yours

Click the settings (⚙) in the find bar to switch effects, themes, and beacon colors. Choose from Anime Laser, Spotlight, Warp Drive, Inferno Flame, Lightning, and Electron Cloud.

Tip: Pin the Oculist extension to your toolbar. Clicking the icon opens a popup where you can toggle it for the current site.
