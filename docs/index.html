How It Works

Three steps to clarity

Oculist replaces the native find UI with its own — same muscle memory, far better visibility.

1

Open the finder

Press ⌘⇧F on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+F on Windows. The Oculist search bar appears in the corner.

2

Type your search

Matches are highlighted as you type. A live count shows how many times your term appears on the page.

3

See it light up

Press Enter to jump between matches. Each active match fires an animated beacon so it's unmissable.
