How It Works

Three steps to clarity

Oculist replaces the native find UI with its own — same muscle memory, far better visibility.

1

Open the finder

Press ⌘⇧F on Mac or Ctrl+Shift+F on Windows. The Oculist search bar appears in the corner.

2

Type your search

Matches are highlighted as you type. A live count shows how many times your term appears on the page.

3

See it light up

Press Enter to jump between matches. Each active match fires an animated beacon so it's unmissable.

Effects

Six ways to be found

Open settings (⚙) inside the find bar to switch effects. Your choice is saved automatically.

Anime Laser

A precision crosshair locks on and a scanning beam sweeps the match.

Spotlight

The surrounding page dims while a halo isolates your match like a stage light.

Warp Drive

A streak of amber light rips across the match at hyperspeed.

Inferno Flame

Fire erupts below the match and burns bright before fading out.

Lightning

A bolt strikes the match in a sharp, flickering flash of blue-white light.

Electron Cloud

Three glowing electrons orbit a bright nucleus at the match site.
